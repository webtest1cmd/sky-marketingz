About Us

Building relationships through quality and trust since our inception.

Who We Are

SKY MARKETINGZ is a premier agricultural trading company based in Kalugumalai, Tamil Nadu. We serve clients across Tamil Nadu, West Bengal, and Bangalore. We specialize in the procurement and supply of high-quality grains and pulses.

With deep roots in the agricultural sector, we bridge the gap between farmers and bulk buyers, ensuring fair pricing and superior quality.

"Our mission is to provide safe, high-quality food products while empowering local farming communities."

Our Core Values

Quality Assurance

Ethical Sourcing

We work directly with farmers to ensure sustainable and ethical procurement practices.

Customer Focus

We believe in long-term partnerships. Your satisfaction is our priority.

Service Regions

We actively supply throughout Tamil Nadu, West Bengal, and Bangalore.

Tuticorin (Thoothukudi)

Known as the "Pearl City," Tuticorin is a major port city and industrial hub. It serves as our primary gateway for coastal distribution and export-import logistics, facilitating efficient sea trade.

Madurai

As the cultural capital and a central geographic hub of Tamil Nadu, Madurai acts as a critical junction for our road transport network, connecting us efficiently to all southern districts.

Tirunelveli

A key agricultural belt known for its paddy fields and rich soil. Our strong presence here allows us to source some of the finest quality grains directly from local farmers.

Virudhunagar

Famous as a business town for oils and pulses. Our operations here focus on the procurement and trading of premium quality oils, dried chilies, and various pulses.

Bangalore

A vital hub for our expanded operations. We supply various feed ingredients including maize and oil cakes to cattle and poultry feed manufacturers in this region.

West Bengal

We cater to the high demand for quality Maize in West Bengal, ensuring consistent supply chains from our major cultivation belts.
